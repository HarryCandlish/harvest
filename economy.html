Cost of living vs wages in New Zealand — who's actually keeping up?

Two numbers that define a generation's financial life

Housing affordability and retirement savings aren't separate policy problems — they're the same squeeze from two directions. High house prices require larger mortgages, which crowd out KiwiSaver contributions. Lower contributions compound into smaller retirement balances. The data tells both halves of that story.

Both pieces are built from primary government and financial datasets. No secondary aggregators, no advocacy group numbers. The figures are what the Reserve Bank, REINZ, and IRD actually report.
